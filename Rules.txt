---------------
Rules of Hearts
---------------

OBJECT OF THE GAME
------------------
To be the player with the lowest score at the end of the game.
When everyone runs out of cards the game ends; and the player with the lowest score wins.

CARD VALUES/SCORING
-------------------
At the end of each hand, players count the number of hearts they have taken as well as the queen of spades.
Hearts count as one point each and the Queen of Spaces counts 13 points.
Each heart - 1 point
The Q of Spades - 13 points
The aggregate total of all scores for each game must be 26 points.

THE DEAL
________
Cards are dealt one at a time, clockwise. In a four-player game, each is dealt 13 cards.

THE PLAY
________
The player holding the 2 of clubs after the pass makes the opening lead.
Each player must follow suit if possible. If a player is void of the suit led, a card of any other suit may be discarded.
The highest card of the suit led wins a trick and the winner of that trick leads next.
